Instructions for the memory game:
By clicking on the "LET'S PLAY" button, you will reach a screen with 16 face down cards. Every 2 random cards have the same ice cream, and the matching pairs must be found. How do you do that? By pressing a card, it turns over and you can see the ice cream. After revealing 2 cards, they are flipped back over and you have to remember which ice cream is in them. When 2 identical cards are clicked, the cards remain visible. We will see you collect as many ice creams as possible!! With pleasure!
